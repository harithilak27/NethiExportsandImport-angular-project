COCO PEAT

     we bring you the natural marvel of coco peat, a sustainable and versatile solution that nurtures both plants and the planet. Rooted in our commitment to eco-friendly alternatives, our coco peat products offer a superior growing medium for a wide range of plants, fostering healthy growth and contributing to a greener, more sustainable future. Derived from the fibrous husk of coconuts, coco peat is a renewable and organic substrate that has revolutionized the way we nurture plants.

COCO PITH

     we bring you the natural excellence of coir pith, a versatile and sustainable solution for gardening and horticulture. Rooted in our commitment to eco-friendly alternatives, our coir pith products offer an organic and nutrient-rich medium to nurture your plants, ensuring optimal growth and contributing to a healthier, more sustainable planet. Derived from the fibrous husk of coconuts, coir pith, also known as coco peat, is a remarkable byproduct with a multitude of benefits for plant cultivation.
