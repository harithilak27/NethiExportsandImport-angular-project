KARUPPU KAVUNI

     Karuppu Kavuni, commonly known as Black Kavuni rice, is a distinctive rice variety originating from the southern regions of India, particularly Tamil Nadu. Renowned for its deep black or purple hue, this aromatic rice carries a unique nutty flavor. Rich in antioxidants and nutrients, Karuppu Kavuni rice not only adds a visually striking element to dishes but also contributes to the preservation of traditional agricultural practices. Its culinary versatility and nutritional benefits make it a cherished and culturally significant grain
