MILLETS

     Embrace wholesome nutrition with our diverse range of millets. Packed with essential nutrients and gluten-free, our millets offer a versatile and healthy alternative for a balanced diet. From foxtail to pearl millet, discover the rich flavors and nutritional benefits that make these ancient grains a smart choice for modern, mindful living.

FOXTAIL MILLETS

      Foxtail millet, scientifically known as Setaria italica, is a nutrient-dense, gluten-free ancient grain that has been cultivated for centuries. Known for its slender, fox-tail-like appearance, this millet is a rich source of essential nutrients such as dietary fiber, iron, and magnesium.

KODO MILLETS

     Kodo millet, scientifically known as Paspalum scrobiculatum, is a resilient and nutritious ancient grain. Renowned for its drought resistance, Kodo millet is a rich source of fiber, protein, and essential nutrients. Its versatile nature allows for various culinary applications, making it a valuable addition to a balanced and health-conscious diet.

LITTLE MILLET

     Little millet, scientifically known as Panicum sumatrense, is a small-grained, gluten-free millet rich in nutrients. Packed with fiber, minerals, and antioxidants, little millets offer excellent digestive benefits and support overall health. With a quick cooking time and a mildly nutty flavor, they are a versatile addition to various dishes, providing a nutritious and satisfying alternative for those seeking diverse and wholesome grains in their diet.

BARNYARD MILLETS

     Barnyard millets, also known as 'Kuthiraivali' or 'Sanwa,' are a gluten-free ancient grain rich in fiber and essential nutrients. Offering a nutty flavor, these millets are a versatile addition to a balanced diet, suitable for various culinary creations like porridge, upma, or as a rice substitute. Embrace the health benefits and wholesome goodness of barnyard millets for a nutritious and flavorful eating experience.
